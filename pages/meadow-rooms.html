Our Meadow Rooms

All guests must provide proof of vaccination upon check-in (C5 for dogs and F3 for cats) and pets must have flea and tick treatment on.

Our Meadow Rooms offer a calm, nature-based stay designed for dogs who enjoy a more open and relaxed environment.

Set within accessible areas of the resort, these rooms provide a comfortable and secure place where dogs can settle in with ease. Natural light, fresh air and a connection to surrounding greenery create an environment that feels easy-going and familiar.

With attentive, individual care provided by our on-site team, every dog is supported in a way that suits their needs and temperament.

The Meadow Rooms are ideal for dogs who thrive in a more social, relaxed atmosphere while still enjoying the comfort and quality of a thoughtfully designed stay.
