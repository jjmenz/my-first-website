Our Canopy Suites

All guests must provide proof of vaccination upon check-in (C5 for dogs and F3 for cats) and pets must have flea and tick treatment on.

Our Canopy Suites offer an elevated stay designed for dogs who deserve a little more space, comfort and calm.

Positioned within our most private areas, each suite provides a quiet, secure environment with generous room to rest and unwind. Soft bedding, climate-controlled interiors, and thoughtfully designed layouts ensure your dog remains comfortable throughout their stay; day and night.

Beyond the suite, guests enjoy access to spacious, protected exercise areas and a carefully balanced routine of play, rest, and individual attention. Our team is on-site around the clock, maintaining a safe and attentive environment where every dog is known and cared for personally.
